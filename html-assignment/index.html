About Me

Welcome to my website!

My name is Zixuan Liu and you can call me Xenia which is my English name. I am from China and this is my first time coming to Canada. But I'm not alone, I came here with my husband named Jason. We just married last year but we have been in love since sophomore year of high school, which means this is our 11 th year together. We also brought our pet from China. His name is Dulu, a British shorthair cat.

I don't know if I am a creative person, but I like music very much. When I was three years old, I started learning piano. And then I also learned how to play guitar, drums, pipa, guzheng, hulusi. Before I went to university, I spend all my after-school time practicing these kinds of musical instruments. However, I didn't have any spare time when I started working. That's why I choose to change a new life in a new country.

Time never goes back to square one, but it can be a new beginning. Zixuan Liu(Xenia)
